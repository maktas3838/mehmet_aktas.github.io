Bio Sketch

  I am an assistant professor and John T. Beresford Endowed Chair in Department of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of Central Oklahoma. My research is on the applications of topological data analysis (TDA) in network science. Particularly, I am interetsed in using TDA in network matching and embedding problems and its applications in social, biological, chemical networks. I received my Ph.D. from the Department of Mathematics at the Florida State University, under the supervision of Eriko Hironaka. I recieved my MS in 2011 from Department of Mathematics at the Bilkent University working with Alex Degtyarev.
